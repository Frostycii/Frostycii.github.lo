About Me

My name is Colin Agraham, better known as FrostyCii. I'm a 16-year-old rapper born on October 13, 2008. I was born in Cape Town, South Africa, and raised in Lesotho.

I started rapping in 2022 because I love hip hop. I'm inspired by artists like Nasty C, Drake, and Pop Smoke.

My Brand - Stormcore

Stormcore is more than a fashion brand — it's a reflection of my energy. I bring the storm when I rap, and that’s what Stormcore stands for. I'm into fashion and I plan to grow this brand into something global.

My Goals

I'm building a career in music, fashion, and big dreams like owning studios, airports, and malls. I want to represent where I come from and inspire the world with my story and talent.
